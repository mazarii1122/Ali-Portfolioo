About Me

Get to know me

Hello! I’m Ali Hassan from Karachi, Pakistan. I completed my F.Sc in Pre-Engineering and I am passionate about building modern, responsive websites.

I have earned a Front-End Developer Certificate from the Pakistan Freelancing Training Program (PFTP) in May 2024. I enjoy turning ideas into interactive and visually appealing web experiences, focusing on clean, efficient, and maintainable code.

My expertise includes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am continuously learning new tools and frameworks to stay up-to-date with the latest trends in web development. I am highly skilled at problem-solving, quickly understanding complex challenges, and achieving my goals efficiently.

Apart from coding, I have a variety of hobbies that keep me balanced and energized: I enjoy playing cricket, working out to maintain focus and health, and traveling and exploring new places to expand my perspectives.

I am committed to continuous learning, collaborating effectively with clients, and delivering digital solutions that not only look great but also provide seamless user experiences. My approach emphasizes professionalism, creativity, and adaptability in every project I take on.
